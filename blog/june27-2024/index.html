Hardbass is a dying music genre. What's the point of listening to it anymore?

June 27, 2024

HARDBASS  is one of my favorite music genres.

ever since "slav" and "gopnik" memes grew AGAIN in popularity around 2017~2018, I've been a follower of the genre. but it seems that there's barely anyone following it anymore.

even the producers are aware..

it's been years since the memes got popular and died off. I haven't talked to anyone that's a furry and also enjoys hardbass as much as i do.

Okay, hang on a minute. What even is "hardbass" anyway?

Hardbass is a subgenre of EDM from Russia around 1990s~2000s.
